Welcome to eclitp.is

Unified REST APIs for astronomical information

Learn how to feed your next astro app
eclipt.is, a service by onekilopars.ec, aims at providing browsable APIs where each piece of astronomical information has its own Uniform Resource Locator (a.k.a. URL). SIMBAD, NED, exoplanet.eu, JPL Horizons, astro telegrams are among the sources of information that eclipt.is intend to unify. Because we believe in the force of integration.
