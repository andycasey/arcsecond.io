About eclipt.is

Eclipt.is is the result of a need that appeared when a colleague of mine convinced me to write iObserve 2.0. That is, a complete overhaul of my app, with new and exciting features, which need to rethink a bit the whole thing. And of of course, it rapidly appeared that if the app could rely on a server/web app which would do part of the job, things could be a lot more flexible. And it rapidly appeared that eclipt.is could actually be interesting for many other people.
