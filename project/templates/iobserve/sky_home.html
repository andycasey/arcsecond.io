Objects

To retrieve all informations about an object, enter its name. Data is retrieved from CDS SIMBAD.

sky/{{ api_version }}/object/


Exoplanets

To retrieve all informations about an exoplanet, enter its name. The parent star name will be included. Data is retrieved from Exomplanet.eu

sky/{{ api_version }}/exoplanet/


Small Bodies

To retrieve all informations about a small body (either an asteroid or a comet), enter its name. Data is retrieved from JPL.

sky/{{ api_version }}/smallbody/
