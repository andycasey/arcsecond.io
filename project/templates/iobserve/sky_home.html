Objects

To retrieve all informations about an object, enter its name. Data is retrieved from CDS Simbad, NASA/IPAC NED and NASA ADS.

Exoplanets

To retrieve all informations about an exoplanet, enter its name. The parent star name will be included. Data is retrieved from Exoplanet.eu

/sky/{{ api_version }}/exoplanet/

Small Bodies

To retrieve all informations about a small body (either an asteroid or a comet), enter its name. Data is retrieved from NASA JPL Horizons.

/sky/{{ api_version }}/smallbody/
